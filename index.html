What is a Watershed?

A watershed is an area of land where all water that falls or flows drains into a common outlet, forming a network of streams and rivers. Our rainforest watersheds are critical ecosystems that:

  • 🌊 Filter and purify drinking water
  • 🌳 Support diverse plant and animal species
  • ☔ Regulate rainfall and prevent flooding
  • 🌍 Combat climate change through carbon sequestration
